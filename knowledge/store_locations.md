## Delivery Information

### Delivery Areas

Each location delivers within a 5-mile radius (except Westside Mall location). Enter your address to check if we deliver to your area.

### Delivery Fees

- Orders under $15: $4.99 delivery fee
- Orders $15 - $30: $2.99 delivery fee
- Orders over $30: FREE delivery

### Delivery Times

- Typical delivery time: 30-45 minutes
- Peak hours (Friday-Saturday 6-8 PM): 45-60 minutes
- Orders placed for future delivery are available up to 3 days in advance
