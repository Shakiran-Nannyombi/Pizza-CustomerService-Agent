# Pizza Policies & FAQ

## Ordering Policy

### How to Order

1. **Online:** Visit <www.pizzaonline.com>
2. **Phone:** Call your nearest location
3. **Mobile App:** Download our app (iOS & Android)
4. **In-Person:** Visit any location

### Payment Methods Accepted

- Cash
- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- Digital Wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay)
- Contoso Pizza Gift Cards
- PayPal (online orders only)

### Order Modifications

- You can modify or cancel your order up to 5 minutes after placing it
- For delivery orders already en route, cancellations may incur a fee
- Call the location directly for urgent modifications

## Dietary Information

### Allergen Information

We work with common allergens including wheat, dairy, soy, and various meats. While we take precautions, we cannot guarantee allergen-free preparation. Please inform us of any severe allergies when ordering.

### Vegetarian Options

- Margherita Pizza
- Veggie Deluxe Pizza
- Truffle Mushroom Pizza
- Garden Salad, Caesar Salad
- Breadsticks, Garlic Knots
- All desserts

### Vegan Options Available Upon Request

- Dairy-free cheese available (+$2.00)
- Vegan crust options
- Multiple vegetable toppings

### Gluten-Free Options

- Gluten-free crust available (+$3.50)
- Prepared in shared kitchen - not recommended for celiac disease

### Nutritional Information

Full nutritional information is available on our website and upon request at any location.

## Refund & Return Policy

### Quality Guarantee

We stand behind our products. If you're not satisfied with your order:

- Contact us within 2 hours of delivery/pickup
- We'll offer a replacement or full refund
- No questions asked

### Damaged or Incorrect Orders

- Take a photo if possible
- Contact the location immediately
- We'll send a replacement or issue a full refund

### Refund Processing Time

- Credit card refunds: 3-5 business days
- Cash refunds: Immediate (in-store)
- Online refunds: 5-7 business days

## Delivery Policy

### Contactless Delivery

Available upon request. Driver will leave order at your door and ring doorbell or knock.

### Delivery Address Requirements

- Must include apartment/unit number if applicable
- Clear directions for hard-to-find locations
- Safe place for driver to park

### Failed Delivery

If we cannot complete delivery (wrong address, no answer, unsafe location):

- We'll attempt to contact you
- Order may be returned to store
- Refund available minus a return fee

### Tips

Tips are appreciated but never required. You can add tips when ordering online or give cash to the driver.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do you offer gluten-free pizza?**  
A: Yes, we offer gluten-free crust for an additional $3.50. Please note it's prepared in a shared kitchen.

**Q: Can I order for future delivery?**  
A: Yes, you can schedule orders up to 3 days in advance on our website or app.

**Q: Do you deliver to hotels?**  
A: Yes! Please provide the hotel name, room number, and phone number.

**Q: What if I'm not home for delivery?**  
A: Please provide special instructions when ordering. We offer contactless delivery where we can leave it at your door.

**Q: Can I get half-and-half pizzas?**  
A: Absolutely! You can split toppings on any pizza.

**Q: Do you have sugar-free drinks?**  
A: Yes, we offer Diet Coke and other diet options.

**Q: How do I join the rewards program?**  
A: Sign up at <www.pizzaonline.com/rewards> or ask at any location.

**Q: Can I order just a half pizza?**  
A: We don't offer half-size pizzas, but our small 10" pizza is perfect for 1-2 people.

**Q: Do you cater events?**  
A: Yes! Our Downtown and East End locations offer catering. Call us 24 hours in advance.

**Q: What are your busiest times?**  
A: Friday and Saturday evenings (6-8 PM) are our peak hours. Order early or schedule ahead!
